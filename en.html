Application and Camp Timeline


1

Application Form & Document Submission

Candidates submit their CV, student certificate, and statement of motivation.

2

Pre-Screening & English Proficiency Test

Candidates who pass the initial stage are given a brief online English test. This assessment comprises two phases:

  • 1. Multiple-Choice Comprehension
  • 2. Speaking Assessment

3

Admissions and Notification

Accepted candidates receive their acceptance notification two weeks prior to the camp commencement.

4

PiA Camp Commencement

A three-day, intensive, in-person project marathon begins at our İzmir office.

5

Project Presentations and Evaluation

Final project presentations are conducted before the jury, and the top intern candidates are selected.
